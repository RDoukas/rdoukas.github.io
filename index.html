Hi! I'm Rebecca Doukas! I recently completed a 12-month coding bootcamp at Actualize to become a software engineer. Originally, I graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Management from Illinois State University and moved to Chicago to pursue a career in Hospitality. I come from a family of programming nerds so it was the next obvious choice for me! Thanks to the change, I finally get their jokes after 30 years of hearing crazy acronyms. I'm also able to throw coding jokes at my brother who I've NEVER made laugh in the past.
