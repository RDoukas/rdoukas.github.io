About Me

I graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Management fromIllinois State University and moved to Chicago to pursue a career in Hospitality. Eight years later and five years at a law firm in Event Management, I realized Hospitality was no longer the career for me.

When brainstorming what career I should switch to I realized at the law firm I found the "techie" AV and computer work to be the most interesting part of my job. Given that I have a real curiosity for technology, I picked up a book on how to code Javascript (it was geared towards kids, no shame) and really enjoyed what I learned. I can't wait to continue to grow my knowledge!

In my free time (when I'm not on COVID lockdown) I enjoy playing pool, throwing darts, cooking, journaling, trying the next new restaurant and spending time with friends and family. I try to spend as much time in Chicago as possible, I love the environment and have so many close friends there. I have a STRONG addiction to coffee which will be apparent with the multiple cups you'll see me chug during class
