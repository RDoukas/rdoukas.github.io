About Me

Hi! I'm Rebecca Doukas! I'm currently a student at Actualize to become a software engineer. Originally, I graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Business Management from Illinois State University and moved to Chicago to pursue a career in Hospitality. Eight years later and five years at a law firm in Event Management, I realized Hospitality was no longer the career for me.

I come from a family of programming nerds so it was the next pick for me! Thanks to the change, I fianlly get their jokes after 30 years of hearing crazy acronyms. I'm also able to through coding jokes at my brother who I've never made laugh in the past.

In my free time I enjoy playing pool, throwing darts, cooking, trying the next new restaurant and spending time with friends and family. I attempt to spend as much time in Chicago as possible, I love the environment and have so many close friends there. I have a STRONG addiction to coffee and love finding new coffee shops.
